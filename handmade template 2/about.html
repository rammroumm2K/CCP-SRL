Why Work With CCP?

Trusted partner

Founded in 1993 by John Hellinckx, CCP has been at the forefront of IT innovation for over 30 years. Starting with Novell Netware networks and evolving through coaxial cable systems to cutting-edge cloud computing, we’ve grown alongside technology to meet the ever-changing needs of businesses like yours.

What Sets Us Apart:

  • Decades of Expertise: With a proven history of adapting to technological advances, we deliver solutions that are both reliable and future-ready.
  • Comprehensive IT Services: From IT infrastructure and cloud solutions to internet and mobile platforms, we cover all aspects of your IT needs.
  • Tailored Solutions: Every business is unique. We provide personalized IT support that aligns with your goals, empowering you to focus on your core mission.
  • Cost-Effective Support: We bridge the gap for organizations that cannot afford a full-time IT specialist by acting as your dedicated IT department—offering premium services at competitive rates.

Who We Serve:

From large enterprises to SMBs and NGOs across Belgium and Europe, CCP is trusted by a diverse range of organizations to manage their IT needs seamlessly.

Our Mission:

To enable your business to thrive by handling the complexities of IT, so you can dedicate your time and resources to growth and profitability.
