ABOUT CCP

Founded in 1993 by John Hellinckx, CCP is a full-service IT provider with a rich history of technological evolution. Starting with Novell Netware networks, we’ve transitioned over the years from coaxial cable systems to modern cloud computing.


Today, we offer comprehensive IT maintenance and services to large enterprises, SMBs, and NGOs across Belgium and Europe. Our mission is to provide tailored IT solutions that empower our clients to focus on their core business goals, driving success, growth, and profitability.


Not every organization can afford an in-house IT specialist—this is where we come in, acting as your dedicated IT department. Our value lies in our ability to fully manage and support your IT needs, delivering high-quality solutions across computer systems, IT infrastructure, internet, cloud, and mobile platforms—all at competitive rates.

Managed IT Service

Our managed IT services allow us to act as your dedicated IT department, providing tailored support that aligns with the criticality of your equipment, system specifics, and budget constraints. Whether you need full IT management or occasional assistance, we offer a service customized to your needs, helping you maintain a reliable and efficient IT infrastructure.

End-User Support

Does your team need daily IT assistance? We offer responsive remote and onsite support for end-users, ensuring they have the help they need to stay productive. Our end-user support service includes troubleshooting, assistance with day-to-day issues, and guidance on best IT practices.

Cloud Migration and Management

Transition to the cloud seamlessly with our migration and management services. We specialize in platforms like Microsoft Azure, Office 365, Google Workspace, AWS, and more. Whether you’re moving to the cloud or need management for an existing setup, we ensure smooth operations, optimal performance, and security for your cloud environment.

Consulting

Need expert advice on your IT setup? Our consulting service offers audits, project implementation support, and long-term strategic planning to enhance your IT infrastructure. We cover areas like IT master plans, helpdesk setup, cloud computing, licensing, and hosting advisory to ensure your IT aligns with your business goals.

Project Management

From planning to execution, our project management service supports your IT initiatives from start to finish. We ensure that each project is completed on time, within budget, and to your specifications, allowing you to focus on core business activities.

Virtualisation

Optimize your resources and improve scalability with our virtualization solutions. We provide expert support in implementing and managing virtual environments, helping you reduce costs, increase efficiency, and enhance flexibility.
